About IEEE Foothill Section

The IEEE Foothill Section strives to enhance the community by advancing technology for humanity. Our Section covers a large geographical area, from West Covina to the end of California on the East, and from Temecula to Baker. The IEEE Foothill Section is part of the Southern Area of IEEE Region 6. In addition, our Section is part of the IEEE Los Angeles Council (LAC). As a Section, we have several volunteers organizing events and activities to inspire, enable, empower, and engage our members.
